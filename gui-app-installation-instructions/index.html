Installing the GUI software

For Windows, Linux (Debian or RPM based distros) you can install and use our GUI software which does the heavy lifting of starting and managing the proxy server, updating your adapter settings to tunnel DNS traffic and applying the required configs; all with a single click.

You can download the GUI software by accessing the Download page on your dashboard.

Once installed you will be presented with a form where you must enter your api key (you can find that under your profile: My account > Profile). We thought it would be easier to copy and paste an API key as opposed to having to remember the username/password combo.

After adding you API key the app will display a small dashboard which lists your connections. You can click on any of the connections, hit Connect and the app will bring up the DnsAdBlock adapter.
